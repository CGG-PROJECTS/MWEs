Project Summary

Age of Acquisition (AoA) for English Multi-Word Expressions

This dataset provides the first large-scale Age of Acquisition norms for over 80,000 English Multi-Word Expressions (MWEs), generated by a GPT-4.1-mini model fine-tuned on crowdsourced human ratings from Prolific (N ≈ 47,163 ratings).

The norms are valuable for psycholinguistic research, NLP, education, and language acquisition studies.
